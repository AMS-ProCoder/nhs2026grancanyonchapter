National Honor Society

The National Honor Society Grand Canyon Chapter 2025-2026 warmly welcomes you to our Student Support project.
This targets three key areas: Academic Support, College & Career Preparation, and a Student Toolkit designed to empower students in their educational journey.
It should be noted that this project was specifically created to aid Stride K12 AZVA High School students, but the resources provided can be beneficial to all students seeking academic excellence and career readiness.
We hope you all find this beneficial, and we wish you the best in your academic and career endeavors!
